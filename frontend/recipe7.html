Chickpea Salad

Ingredients:

½ cup boiled chickpeas, 1 small tomato, ½ cucumber, 1 tsp lemon juice, salt & pepper

Steps:

Chop tomato and cucumber.
Mix with chickpeas, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.

Nutrition:

Calories: ~180 kcal

Protein: ~9 g

Carbs: ~25 g

Fats: ~5 g

Fiber: ~6 g

Meal Type: Lunch / Snack
