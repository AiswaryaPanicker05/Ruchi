Microwave Mug Cake

Ingredients:

4 tbsp flour, 2 tbsp sugar, 2 tbsp milk, 1 tbsp oil, ½ tsp baking powder, cocoa powder (optional)

Steps:

Mix all ingredients in a microwave-safe mug.
Microwave 1–2 mins till cooked.

Nutrition:

Calories: ~250 kcal

Protein: ~5 g

Carbs: ~30 g

Fats: ~12 g

Fiber: ~2 g

Meal Type: Dessert / Snack
