Fruit Yogurt Bowl

Ingredients:

½ cup yogurt, ½ cup mixed fruits (apple, banana, berries), 1 tsp honey

Steps:

Mix yogurt and honey.
Add chopped fruits on top.

Nutrition:

Calories: ~120 kcal

Protein: ~5 g

Carbs: ~20 g

Fats: ~2 g

Fiber: ~3 g

Meal Type: Snack / Breakfast
