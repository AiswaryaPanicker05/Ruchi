Ruchi Recipes

1. Masala Omelette
⭐⭐⭐⭐☆

Ingredients:

2 eggs, 1 small onion (chopped), 1 small tomato (chopped), green chili (optional), salt & pepper, 1 tsp oil

Steps:

Beat eggs with salt and pepper.
Add chopped onion, tomato, and chili.
Heat oil in a pan, pour the mixture, cook both sides till golden.

Time: 5–7 mins | Servings: 1 | Meal Type: Breakfast / Snack

2. Quick Vegetable Stir-Fry
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Ingredients:

1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, capsicum), 1 tsp oil, ½ tsp soy sauce, salt to taste

Steps:

Heat oil in a pan.
Add vegetables, sauté for 3–4 mins.
Add soy sauce and salt, cook 1–2 mins more.

Time: 7 mins | Servings: 1–2 | Meal Type: Lunch / Dinner

3. Banana Peanut Butter Toast
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Ingredients:

1 slice bread, 1 tbsp peanut butter, ½ banana sliced

Steps:

Toast bread lightly.
Spread peanut butter.
Top with banana slices.

Time: 3–4 mins | Servings: 1 | Meal Type: Breakfast / Snack

4. Vegetable Upma
⭐⭐⭐⭐☆

Ingredients:

½ cup semolina, 1 cup chopped veggies (carrot, beans), 1 tsp oil, ½ tsp mustard seeds, salt to taste, 1–2 cups water

Steps:

Roast semolina lightly, set aside.
Heat oil, add mustard seeds and veggies, sauté 2–3 mins.
Add water and salt, bring to boil.
Add semolina gradually, stir till thick.

Time: 10 mins | Servings: 1–2 | Meal Type: Breakfast

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Time: 2 mins | Servings: 1 | Meal Type: Snack / Breakfast

6. Microwave Mug Cake
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Ingredients:

4 tbsp flour, 2 tbsp sugar, 2 tbsp milk, 1 tbsp oil, ½ tsp baking powder, cocoa powder (optional)

Steps:

Mix all ingredients in a microwave-safe mug.
Microwave 1–2 mins till cooked.

Time: 5 mins | Servings: 1 | Meal Type: Dessert / Snack

7. Chickpea Salad
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Ingredients:

½ cup boiled chickpeas, 1 small tomato, ½ cucumber, 1 tsp lemon juice, salt & pepper

Steps:

Chop tomato and cucumber.

Time: 5 mins | Servings: 1 | Meal Type: Lunch / Snack

8. Masala Peanut Butter Sandwich
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Ingredients:

2 slices bread, 1 tbsp peanut butter, ¼ tsp chili powder, salt to taste, chopped cucumber/tomato

Steps:

Spread peanut butter on bread.
Sprinkle chili powder, add veggies, cover with other slice.

Time: 3–4 mins | Servings: 1 | Meal Type: Snack / Breakfast

9. Idiyappam with Coconut Milk
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Ingredients:

Idiyappam (store-bought or fresh), ½ cup coconut milk, ½ tsp sugar, pinch of salt

Steps:

Steam idiyappam for 2–3 mins.
Mix coconut milk with sugar and salt.
Pour over idiyappam and serve.

Time: 5–7 mins | Servings: 1–2 | Meal Type: Breakfast / Snack
