Quick Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, capsicum), 1 tsp oil, ½ tsp soy sauce, salt to taste

Steps:

Heat oil in a pan.
Add vegetables, sauté for 3–4 mins.
Add soy sauce and salt, cook 1–2 mins more.

Nutrition:

Calories: ~100 kcal

Protein: ~3 g

Carbs: ~12 g

Fats: ~5 g

Fiber: ~4 g

Meal Type: Lunch / Dinner
