Corn Chaat

Ingredients:

1 cup boiled corn, 1 small onion (chopped), 1 small tomato (chopped), lemon juice, chaat masala to taste

Time:

5 mins

Nutrition:

Calories: ~120 kcal

Protein: ~5 g

Carbs: ~25 g

Fats: ~2 g

Fiber: ~3 g

Meal Type:

Snack / Evening

Quick Peanut Butter Sandwich

Ingredients:

2 slices bread, 1 tbsp peanut butter, a pinch of chili powder (optional)

Time:

3-4 mins

Nutrition:

Calories: ~210 kcal

Protein: ~7 g

Carbs: ~25 g

Fats: ~10 g

Fiber: ~3 g

Meal Type:

Snack / Breakfast

Instant Oats Overnight / Cold Oats

Ingredients:

½ cup oats, ½ cup milk or curd, fruits (banana/apple), honey

Steps:

Mix all ingredients and soak for 5–10 mins (or overnight in fridge if available)

Nutrition:

Calories: ~180 kcal

Protein: ~6 g

Carbs: ~30 g

Fats: ~4 g

Fiber: ~5 g

Meal Type:

Breakfast / Snack
