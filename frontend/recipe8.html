Masala Peanut Butter Sandwich

Ingredients:

2 slices bread, 1 tbsp peanut butter, ¼ tsp chili powder, salt to taste, chopped cucumber/tomato

Steps:

Spread peanut butter on bread.
Sprinkle chili powder, add veggies, cover with other slice.

Nutrition:

Calories: ~210 kcal

Protein: ~7 g

Carbs: ~25 g

Fats: ~10 g

Fiber: ~3 g

Meal Type: Snack / Breakfast
