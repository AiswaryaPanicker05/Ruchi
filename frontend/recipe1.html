Masala Omelette

Ingredients:

2 eggs, 1 small onion (chopped), 1 small tomato (chopped), green chili (optional), salt & pepper, 1 tsp oil

Steps:

Beat eggs with salt and pepper.
Add chopped onion, tomato, and chili.
Heat oil in a pan, pour the mixture, cook both sides till golden.

Nutrition:

Calories: ~180 kcal

Protein: ~12 g

Carbs: ~2 g

Fats: ~14 g

Fiber: ~1 g

Meal Type: Breakfast / Snack
