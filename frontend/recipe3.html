Banana Peanut Butter Toast

Ingredients:

1 slice bread, 1 tbsp peanut butter, ½ banana sliced

Steps:

Toast bread lightly.
Spread peanut butter.
Top with banana slices.

Nutrition:

Calories: ~200 kcal

Protein: ~6 g

Carbs: ~28 g

Fats: ~8 g

Fiber: ~3 g

Meal Type: Breakfast / Snack
